Target Audience:

This site will be targeting primarily anyone, but will cater to those who want to see what the weather is like without having to look very hard. The sites focus is being very simple and easy to read at a glance. To be very simple for anyone to read and understand will help out everyone in the community to be aware of the weather and saftey conditions outside.

Name: John Smith

Job: Construction business owner

Demographics: 56 years old, Married, Grandfather, Homeowner

Reason to Visit:Because he owns a construction company in town, he will need to know what the weather is like at any given time in case work needs to halt or can continue.

Names: George and Jess

Jobs: Retired Couple

Demographics: late 70s, Retired, Difficulty understanding technology and computers, Take walks with eachother outside

Reason to Visit:They enjoy talking walks with eachother to stay healthy. Before they go out they would like to know the weather to know how to dress and to know if they will be able to go out that day.

Scenarios:

1) I just want to know what the temperature is outside.

A: You can easily open the site and get to know the temperature and much other informationn at just a few clicks.

2) I want to check and see if I need a jacket.

A: This will also be able to see and find quickly. You will be able to see the weather conditions with just a few clicks.

3) Will I be able to see traffic information as well?

A: No, this site will have information on articles and stories of the towns, but it will not have updates traffic information.

4) Can I see the weather for other days rather than the current day?

A: Yes you will be able to see the weeks projected weather assesments all together.

5) Will there be a map of the weather to look at?

A: Yes there will be a updated map of the area with the weather information to view.

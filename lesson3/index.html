Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to provide updated and accurate weather information on the following places.

  • Preston
  • Soda Springs
  • Fish Haven
  • Storm Center

Easy Weather Info is also here to provide weather stories on the above towns, provide images of the weather forcasts as well as what the weather looks like in those current areas. The goal of the site is to help the public in those areas prepare for future weather events as well as know what it is at the current time. The site will also provide town information and relevant articles.

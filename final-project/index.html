All Day Adventure

Length: The length of this trip is the longest of the trips. It is 4.2 miles long.

Skill Level: The skill level of this trip is very beginner. It is for all ages from 13 and up.

Cost: The cost of this trip is $29.99 a Person.

Description: This trip is the easiest of all the trips and is the longest. It consists of mostly small rapids and smooth water. This trip is recomended for those who are just learning how to raft or is their first time.

Rock the Boat

Length: The length of this trip is 2.1 Miles

Skill Level: This is a trip recomended for experienced rafters who have done a few trips before and know the ropes.

Cost: The cost of this trip is $29.99 a Person

Description: This trip consists of both calm waters and some intense rapids. We recomend that you have done a few trips before so that when it comes to the big rapids you know what to do already so you can be safe.

Spartan Test

Length: The length of this trip matches the name, and is 6 Miles long

Skill Level: This trip is designed for the expert riders.

Cost: $50.00 a Person

Description: This trip consists of large and somewhat dangerous rapids as well as a long distance. This is for the riders who know what they are doing and are looking for a challenge!!
